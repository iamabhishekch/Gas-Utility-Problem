Account Management

How do I update my account information?

You can update your personal information by visiting your profile page.

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to your profile and select the "Change Password" option.

Service Requests

How do I submit a new service request?

You can submit a new service request by clicking on the "Submit New Service Request" button on your dashboard or directly from the service request form.

How can I track my service requests?

All your service requests are listed on your dashboard with their current status. You can click on any request to view more details.

What documentation should I include with my request?

When submitting a service request, you can attach relevant photos, documents, or other files that might help our team understand and address your issue more efficiently.

Emergency Services

Gas Leak or Emergency?

Do not use this online portal for emergencies.

If you smell gas or suspect a leak, please:

  1. Leave the area immediately
  2. Call our emergency line: 1-800-GAS-LEAK (1-800-427-5325)
  3. Do not return until the area has been declared safe
